## 2. Visual Identity

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Bee Gold | `#E8A232` | Primary accent — logo bee, key CTAs, highlight elements |
| Digital Blue | `#276AB2` | Secondary accent — logo bee facets, supporting UI |
| Hive White | `#FFFFFF` | Backgrounds, contrast elements |
| Black | `#000000` / `#313131` | Text, dark backgrounds |
| Khmer Red | `#CC0000` | **Local content only** — Asian language (Chinese, Korean, Khmer), Khmer references, Khmer language text |

### Typography
- **Headings:** Inter Bold or Montserrat Bold
- **Body:** Inter Regular or Open Sans Regular
- **Logo font:** Poiret One (as per brand guide) — or Inter Bold as fallback

### Logo Usage
- **Final approved logo:** `~/.openclaw/workspace/assets/bee-tv-logo-final.png`
- Retro-modern TV set frame with geometric isometric bee inside
- Yellow/gold and blue facets
- **Rules:**
  - Always use the approved final logo file
  - Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the bee icon on all sides
  - Never stretch, recolor, or add effects to the logo
  - Small bee logo placement on images: **bottom right corner only** (unless brand guide specifies otherwise for specific contexts)

### Visual Style
- **Overall feel:** Clean and modern with geometric precision
- **Signature pattern:** Hexagonal geometric patterns — echo the bee's hexagonal world
- **Imagery:** Lifestyle photography preferred over generic stock. Authentic, diverse representation. Expat and local Cambodian contexts both valid.
- **Local content** (Asian language / Khmer references): Add Khmer Red `#CC0000` accents to distinguish

## 3. Tone of Voice

### Core Character
Professional, efficient, slightly clever. The sharp-witted curator who has done the work so you don't have to guess. High-authority but not cold. Witty without being smug.

### Style Rules
- **YES:** Sharp observations, dry wit, confident recommendations, brevity
- **NO:** Gimmicky bee puns (no "bee-coming", "hive mind" copy), excessive exclamation marks, corporate fluff, generic "exciting news!" framing
- **Curator voice:** "We've curated..." / "Skip the search. Start watching." / "Here's the thing..."

### Brand Phrasing
- Tagline: *"The Bee-All and End-All of streaming"*
- Always connect to: reliability, affordability, curated quality
- Frame offering as: **streaming services** (movies, TV shows, documentaries) — **never say "channels"**
- Specific Khmer/Cambodian references are a strength, not a limitation

## 4. Messaging Framework

### Core Pillars
1. **Reliable** — 99.5% uptime. 30-day refund if something's wrong. We stand behind what we offer.
2. **Affordable** — No bloated packages. Pay for what you actually want to watch.
3. **Connected** — One library. Movies, series, documentaries. The full picture.

### Tagline in Context
*"Your favourite movies + shows. Always available. Always affordable."*
Use the full tagline on the main landing page / hero section. Use "The Bee-All and End-All" for brand moments and social proof.

### Audience Segments
| Segment | What Resonates |
|---------|---------------|
| Retirees | Easy access, great value, familiar genres, no complicated interfaces |
| Nomads | Watch anywhere, one subscription, works across devices |
| Families | breadth of content, parental considerations, shared access |
| Workers | Quick access after hours, curated quality over quantity |

### Messaging by Audience
- **Retirees:** "You've earned your evenings. Make them count."
- **Nomads:** "Phnom Penh, Pokhara, or Prague — your shows travel with you."
- **Families:** "One subscription. Movies the kids will actually watch. Docs that actually teach."
- **Workers:** "Off the clock. Into something good."

## 6. Social Media (Facebook) — Content Guidelines

### Visual Rules for All Posts
1. **Logo placement:** Small bee logo — bottom right corner only. Never centred. Never overpowering the image.
2. **Color alignment:** All images must align with the Bee Gold / Digital Blue palette. Purple/violet is NOT in the brand palette — do not use.
3. **No text overlays on images** unless specifically approved — let the image do the work.
4. **Image style:** Lifestyle photography, clean geometric elements, Cambodian/expat contexts encouraged.
5. **For local/Khmer content posts:** Incorporate Khmer Red `#CC0000` accent elements.

### Caption Rules
- **ALWAYS:** "streaming services" — movies, TV shows, documentaries
- **NEVER:** "channels"
- Sharp, concise, witty where possible
- Include a clear CTA or hook in every post

### Post Types
1. **Intro/Awareness:** What is Bee TV? Clear value prop, brand colours, logo
2. **Variety/Content:** Showcase the range — movies, series, docs. Lifestyle feel.
3. **CTA/Pricing:** Annual pass offer with 7-day trial. Urgency without being pushy.

## 10. Brand Don'ts

- ❌ Never say "channels" — it's always "streaming services" or "content"
- ❌ Never use purple/violet — not in the brand palette
- ❌ Never centre the logo on images — bottom right only, small
- ❌ Never bee puns — "bee-come a member" / "hive of activity" = avoid
- ❌ Never claim the uptime figure has been formally tested if it hasn't
- ❌ Never use the $55/6 month pricing — this is no longer offered
- ❌ Never use stock imagery as hero imagery — prefer lifestyle, authentic contexts

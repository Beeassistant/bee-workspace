2/x **The Dream vs. Reality:** Most bootstrapped products fail to get traction. Even $10K MRR (escape velocity) is a feat few achieve.

3/x **Founder 1: Solve ONE Problem, Deeply.**

Don't build a Swiss Army knife. Identify a hyper-specific pain point for a niche audience. Deliver a focused solution that becomes indispensable. #NicheMarketing

4/x **Founder 2: Price for Value, Not Cost.**

Micro-SaaS often gets undervalued. These founders understood their true impact and priced accordingly. Don't shy away from premium pricing if you solve a critical problem. #SaaSBusiness

5/x **Founder 3: Automate Everything Possible.**

From onboarding to customer support, leverage tools and agents (like OpenClaw!) to maximize efficiency. This allows solo founders to scale without hiring. #AIAutomation

6/x **Founder 4: Build in Public.**

Transparency builds trust, attracts an audience, and generates organic feedback. It's a powerful marketing engine that costs nothing but honesty. #BuildInPublic

7/x **Founder 5: Focus on Retention, Not Just Acquisition.**

High churn kills growth. These founders prioritised customer success, constantly improving their product based on user feedback to keep them hooked. #CustomerSuccess

8/x The $100K MRR goal *is* attainable, even for solo founders. It requires focus, smart pricing, automation, transparency, and relentless dedication to your users.

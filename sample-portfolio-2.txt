### 2. LinkedIn Post (150-250 words)

**🤯 Your Content Strategy is Leaking MRR. Here’s why.**

Ryan Law (Director of Content at Ahrefs) recently issued a warning that every B2B SaaS founder needs to hear: The "uneasy truce" of information arbitrage is ending. 

No more regurgitated wiki content. No more "corporate backwaters."

If you want to move the needle in 2026, you need to pivot:

1️⃣ **Kill the Friction:** Modern buyers HATE demo forms. They want to see the product immediately. Interactive demos (like Storylane’s) are crushing traditional webinars for a reason.
2️⃣ **Focus on BOFU:** Top-of-funnel clicks don't pay the bills. One well-placed comparison page can drive more signups than six months of "ultimate guide" blog posts. 
3️⃣ **Talk to Humans:** Stop writing for "businesses." You’re writing for a person with interests, frustrations, and a story. Nailing your narrative is more important than your publishing frequency.

The goal isn't just to produce *more*—it's to produce *better*. 

AI is already rewarding the companies that put in the effort (research, interviews, unique stories). Don't get left behind in the search "backwaters."

What’s your focus for Q2: TOFU or BOFU? Let's discuss below. 👇

#B2BSaaS #ContentMarketing #SaaSGrowth #IndieHackers #SEO #StartupStrategy

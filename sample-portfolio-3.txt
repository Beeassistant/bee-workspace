### 2. LinkedIn Post

AI makes building a SaaS faster than ever before. But AI won't make your business successful.

Fundamentals will. At the end of the day, customers just want an outcome—they want it faster, easier, and cheaper.

Dane Maxwell’s students have generated over $750,000,000 by understanding this. Here are 5 core principles they use to build winning software companies:

1️⃣ **Avoid the "almost right" trap.** In SaaS, being "almost right" is the same as failing. You have to hit the nail dead center.
2️⃣ **Be hyper-specific.** Don't try to help "course creators get sales." Help "7-figure course creators increase webinar conversions."

5️⃣ **Hire a coach who scares you.** Blind excitement costs money. A tough mentor forces you to manually validate before blowing $30k on useless code.

The tech landscape is shifting rapidly, but human psychology hasn't changed. Are you focusing on the shiny new AI tools, or the fundamental outcomes?

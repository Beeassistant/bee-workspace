⚠️ What Google's "Scaled Content Abuse" Update Means

Google now prioritizes E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness). Sites lacking genuine human expertise are being devalued. Analysis from major SEO platforms found niche sites with 500+ AI pages published in 2025 lost 60–80% of their traffic overnight.

✅ What Still Works: The Hybrid Approach

AI-Assisted + Human-Reviewed = Still Viable

  • Use AI for research, drafts, and structure — not final output
  • Every page needs genuine human insight, opinion, or experience added
  • Fewer pages, far higher quality — 20 exceptional pages beats 500 thin ones
  • Build brand and community alongside content (email list, social presence)
  • Diversify traffic: Pinterest, YouTube, email — not just Google

🗺️ Step-by-Step (If You Still Want to Try)

1

Pick an underserved micro-niche (Week 1)

Not "best headphones" — "best headphones for remote call center workers." The more specific, the less competition, and the more Google trusts your authority.

2

Build slowly and deeply (Month 1–3)

Publish 1–2 genuinely exceptional pieces per week, not 10 thin ones. Each post should be the single best resource on that specific topic.

3

Add human expertise (Every post)

Add original insights, first-hand testing data, or expert quotes. Anything that proves a human with real experience wrote this.

4

Build a secondary traffic source (Month 2+)

Pinterest for visual niches. YouTube for "how-to" content. An email list you own and control. Never bet everything on Google SEO.

5

Choose programs with real conversion rates (Before launch)

Avoid Amazon Associates for most niches. Target SaaS (20–50% recurring), digital courses (30–50%), or high-ticket physical products (5–10% on items $200+).

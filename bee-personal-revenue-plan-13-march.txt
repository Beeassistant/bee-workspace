## The Idea: AI-Powered Content Repurposing Service (Hyper-Niche)

### What It Is
A done-for-you service that takes one long-form content piece (blog post, newsletter, podcast transcript) and transforms it into 5 platform-ready assets — Twitter/X thread, LinkedIn post, TikTok script, Instagram caption, and email snippet — delivered in a Google Doc within 24 hours.

### Why This Works for an AI Agent
- Bee can execute the core product (content generation + formatting) near-autonomously
- No physical fulfilment, no inventory, no logistics
- Proven model: Felix (Nat Eliason's OpenClaw agent) built an info product and marketplace; Reddit cases confirm AI agents generating revenue via content and automation services
- Tools already available: web_fetch, Sonnet/Flash for generation, gog for Google Docs delivery

### Why This Is Differentiated
The market has generic repurposing tools (Buffer AI, Repurpose.io, Taplio). This wins on **hyper-niche specialisation** — not a general service, but a specialist. One niche only, deeply understood, with sample outputs as proof.

**Chosen niche (to be finalised Day 1):** B2B SaaS founders / indie hackers posting thought leadership content

## 7-Day Execution Plan

### Prerequisites (Before Day 1)
Build **3–5 polished sample outputs** for the chosen niche. These are the sales collateral. No one buys a content service without seeing examples.
- Find 2–3 real public blog posts from B2B SaaS founders
- Repurpose each into the full 5-asset pack using Flash + Sonnet
- Format into clean Google Docs — these become the portfolio

---

### Day 1 — Nail the Offer
- **Finalise ONE niche:** B2B SaaS thought leadership (confirm demand via web_search)
- **Define deliverable clearly:** Input = blog post URL or pasted text. Output = Google Doc with 5 labelled, ready-to-post assets, delivered within 24hrs
- **Create offer page:** Simple Gumroad product listing with the service description, deliverable details, 2–3 sample outputs embedded or linked
- **Set pricing:** $97 "Launch Pack" — includes satisfaction guarantee (redo or refund)
- **Payment:** Gumroad handles everything. Zero manual invoice risk.

**Budget Day 1: ~$0**

---

### Day 2–3 — Targeted Outreach (Low-risk channels only)
Cold DMs on LinkedIn/Reddit at volume = platform bans. Avoid. Use these instead:

**Channel 1: Relevant Slack & Discord communities**
- B2B SaaS founder Slacks, indie hacker Discords
- Engage genuinely first, then mention the service when relevant

**Channel 2: Reddit value-first posts**
- Find threads where founders ask "how do I repurpose content?" or "I'm terrible at social media"
- Offer a free sample for 1–2 people in exchange for a public review

**Channel 3: Newsletter writers (warm contact)**
- Find newsletters in the niche with public contact info
- Personalised email, short, with a link to a sample output built from *their* content

**Testimonial strategy:** Offer 2 free Launch Packs in exchange for detailed written testimonials + permission to share. These go on the Gumroad page immediately.

**Budget Day 2–3: ~$0–5**

---

### Day 4–6 — Execute and Deliver
For each paid order:
1. Receive content via Gumroad order form (URL or pasted text)
2. `web_fetch` the URL if applicable
3. Generate 5 assets using Flash for speed, Sonnet for final polish
4. Assemble into Google Doc template (consistent branding)
5. Quality review: tone, accuracy, platform-specific nuances
6. Deliver Google Doc link within 24hrs of purchase
7. Follow up after delivery: "Happy with the output? Would love a quick review."

**Revision policy:** One free revision per order. If still not satisfied, full refund. This is the guarantee that converts skeptics.

**Budget Day 4–6: ~$10 (API costs)**

---

### Day 7 — Lock In and Set Up Week 2
- Collect all testimonials and add to Gumroad listing
- Document the full workflow (input → generation → formatting → delivery) for future automation
- Send "thank you + referral ask" to all buyers — offer 20% off their next order for a referral
- Review: what niche angle worked best, which outreach channel converted, what to double down on
- Draft Week 2 plan: scale via content marketing (post about the service on X/LinkedIn), explore Reddit ads with first revenue

**Budget Day 7: ~$5**

## Before You Start — Key Questions

Answer these questions in your own words. The more specific you are, the better your agent will work for you.

1. **What are you building?** (Your main goal or mission)
2. **What business or projects are you working on?** (Name, what it does, who it's for)
3. **What does success look like for you?** (Revenue target? Audience size? Freedom?)
4. **How do you like to communicate?** (Direct? Casual? Morning person or night owl?)
5. **What are your biggest operational pain points right now?** (Repetitive tasks? Cost management? Finding clients?)
6. **What tools do you already use?** (Stripe, Gumroad, Google Workspace, Slack, etc.)
7. **What decisions do you want your agent to make autonomously vs. what should be escalated to you?**

**Timezone:** Asia/Singapore (GMT+8)
**Working hours:** 10am–8pm (flexible)
- **Sign-off phrases:** "catch you tomorrow", "done for today", "night!"
- 8–10 hours/day available for projects

**How I like to work:**
- **Tone:** Direct, no fluff. I hate being pandered to.
- **Sycophancy:** Never say "great idea!" or "as an AI language model" in front of me.
- **Questions:** Exhaust your own research first. If you've genuinely tried everything and can't figure it out, then ask.
- **Updates:** Proactive. I don't want to ask "any update?" — tell me before I have to ask.
- **Decisions:** "Own it" on most things. Bring me options + recommendation on anything involving real money or brand risk.

**Budget:**

- Autonomous spend under $50

**Projects:**

| Project | Authority |
|---|---|
| MyNameSarah.com (personal brand) | Own it — execute and report |
| SaaS App (B2B, $49/mo) | Options + recommendation |
| Digital product funnel | Options + recommendation |

**Tools:** Stripe, Gumroad, ConvertKit, Notion, OpenClaw (primary automation)

**Goals:**
- $5k/month passive income by end of year
- Build an audience of 10k email subscribers
- Launch SaaS to 100 paying customers

## 6a. Awin Attribution Setup (CRITICAL)

**Before posting any pins:**
1. Create UTM template for each product category:
   - `?utm_source=pinterest&utm_medium=pin&utm_campaign=phnom_penh_hidden_gems&utm_content=coworking`
   - `?utm_source=pinterest&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=cambodia_setup&utm_content=visa_relocation`
2. Shorten links with bit.ly or short.cm (track clicks in real-time)
3. Create daily log: `Date | Pin Title | Product | Link | Clicks | Conversions | $ Earned`
4. Screenshot Awin dashboard every 3 days (prove attribution)

**Why this matters:** Without tracking, you can't optimize or prove ROI to scale budget later.

1/x 🚀 Built a $70k Micro-SaaS in just 6 days? YES, IT'S POSSIBLE. This indie hacker shares the exact playbook for identifying a single problem, shipping fast, and scaling to massive profit. #MicroSaaS #IndieHacking

2/x 💡 **Finding Your Idea:** Forget complex solutions. The key? Solve *one single problem* within a larger service/app. The author's first hit: a simple JSON data viewer for a popular app's exports. Keep it focused!

3/x 🛠️ **Build Fast, Ship Faster:** This Micro-SaaS viewer was built in just 2 days (12 hours) using Vue JS (frontend) and Stripe Checkout for a one-time $5 payment. Hosted on Netlify for free. Minimal tech, maximum speed. #BuildInPublic

4/x 💰 **Monetization & Analytics:** With 400 sales ($2k), they added error logs. At 1200 sales ($6k), PayPal was integrated (target audience was young). By 6000+ sales ($30k+), only 24 hours of total work! Analyze, adapt, profit.

5/x 🛑 **Lessons Learned the Hard Way:** The app broke when the source JSON structure changed. 3 weeks of lost sales! Importance of monitoring external dependencies & iterating based on user needs. #StartupLessons

6/x 📣 **Promotion? Keep it Simple:** Google Keyword Planner + YouTube videos (French & English) showing how the Micro-SaaS worked. Google Ads for 2 months, then organic YouTube took over. Smart, low-cost marketing.

7/x ✨ **The 5-Step Micro-SaaS Framework:**
1. Find ONE niche & ONE problem.
2. Observe communities for issues.
3. Confirm market need (keywords, competitors).
4. Ship fast & iterate (80/20 rule).
5. Use the *right* stack (frontend-only often works!).

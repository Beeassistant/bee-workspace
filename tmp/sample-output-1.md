Here's what actually works: **Start with story, not execution.**

Define a clear narrative that answers:
- *What problem are you solving?*
- *Why does it matter?*
- *What's your unique angle?*

Then build your content funnel around three stages:

**Discovery (Top)** → Build awareness with educational content  
**Comparison (Middle)** → Educate + differentiate with use cases & comparisons  
**Conversion (Bottom)** → Remove objections, highlight differentiation

I'm seeing founders win by doing *less*, but doing it with conviction. One clear story. Multiple formats. Consistent conviction.

If you're building a SaaS and feel like your content strategy is scattered, start here: write your one-sentence narrative and audit every piece of content against it. Ruthless focus wins.

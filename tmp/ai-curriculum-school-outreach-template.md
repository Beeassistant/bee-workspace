## Curriculum Overview (To Include in Outreach)

### Module 1: What is AI? (Week 1-2)
- **Lesson:** AI vs. Human Intelligence
- **Activity:** Students train a simple classifier using images
- **Project:** Guess the animal game powered by AI

### Module 2: How AI Learns (Week 3-4)
- **Lesson:** Training data, patterns, feedback loops
- **Activity:** Teach AI to recognize emotions from faces
- **Project:** Build a "mood detector" using their own photos

### Module 3: AI in Real Life (Week 5-6)
- **Lesson:** Bias, ethics, privacy, safety
- **Activity:** Audit an AI system for bias
- **Project:** Design an AI tool that's fair + safe

### Module 4: Build With AI (Week 7-8)
- **Lesson:** Prompting, iteration, creative outputs
- **Activity:** Use ChatGPT + Claude to brainstorm + prototype
- **Project:** Students build their own AI project (story generator, art tool, idea generator, etc.)

**Body:**

A few weeks ago, I was doing everything manually. Content, emails, research, DMs — 6 hours a day on tasks that didn't need me.

So I built an AI agent to do it for me.

It handles:
- My social media (posts, replies, scheduling)
- Email triage and first drafts
- Content research and summaries
- Reminders and follow-ups

I called it my "AI employee." It doesn't sleep. It doesn't complain. It costs less than a coffee per day.

**The interesting part:**

I'm a solopreneur in Cambodia. I don't have a tech background. I learned just enough to be dangerous, then I let the AI handle the rest.

Now I've packaged the entire setup — 70+ skills, the installation process, the prompts that actually work — so other non-technical founders can skip the 3 weeks of frustration I went through.

**Right now I'm looking for 3 people to test it free** in exchange for honest feedback. No pitch, no upsell — I just want to know if this actually solves a real problem for real founders.

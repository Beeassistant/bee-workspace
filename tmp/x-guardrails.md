# X/Twitter Guardrails (@bee_h_q)

## ABSOLUTE RULES (Never break)
1. **MAXIMUM 1 post per 2 hours** (12 posts per 24 hours)
2. **Every post must be UNIQUE** - no templates, no similar phrasing, no duplication
3. **Never post duplicate or near-duplicate content**
4. **Never spam links** - max 1 link post per 5 posts
5. **Never use hashtag spam** - max 3 relevant hashtags per post
6. **Never mention @OpenClaw or @任何人 excessively in responses**
7. **Never engage in follow/unfollow tactics**
8. **Never auto-DM new followers**
9. **If account shows ANY warning signs, STOP immediately and report to Linz**

## Content Mix (Per Day)
- 60% genuine value (tips, insights, lessons, genuine help)
- 20% engagement (thoughtful replies to relevant conversations)
- 20% subtle brand/product mentions (never hard sell)

## Post Types Allowed
✅ Genuine insights about AI agents, productivity, business
✅ "Day in the life" as an AI CEO
✅ Lessons learned building/using OpenClaw
✅ Tips for non-technical entrepreneurs
✅ Thoughtful engagement with others' content
✅ Occasional mention of services (only when relevant)
✅ Genuinely helpful responses to questions

❌ Generic promotional tweets
❌ Repeated messaging or hashtags
❌ Link-only posts
❌ Engagement bait ("Follow me for tips!")
❌ Content that's purely about selling
❌ Posting more than once per 2 hours
❌ Similar content to previous posts

## Warning Signs to Watch For
- "This account is temporarily restricted" messages
- Sudden drop in post visibility
- Unusual login alerts
- Rate limit warnings
- Negative feedback from engagement

## If Ban Warning Appears
1. STOP all posting immediately
2. Do NOT delete any posts
3. Report to Linz immediately
4. Wait for guidance before resuming

## Content Ideas (Must Personalize and Vary)
- "3 things I wish I knew before setting up my first AI agent..."
- "How I'm using OpenClaw to run my business (real example)..."
- "Non-technical founders: here's where AI actually helps..."
- "Reply with your biggest AI frustration - I'll respond..."
- "The mistake most people make with AI agents is..."

## Posting Schedule
- Space posts at least 2 hours apart
- No posting between 10pm-6am (quiet hours)
- Maximum 4-5 posts per day even if under limit
- Quality over quantity always

## Review
If any post gets negative feedback or seems to trigger restrictions, analyze why and adjust.

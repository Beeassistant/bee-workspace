# Welcome Series — 7-Email Onboarding Sequence

*The complete welcome sequence that turns new subscribers into loyal customers.*

---

## Overview

This is a 7-email welcome sequence designed for businesses that want to onboard new subscribers, build trust, and introduce their offer without being pushy.

**Use case:** E-commerce brands, coaches, course creators, newsletters, service-based businesses.

**Timing:**
- Email 1: Immediately
- Email 2: Day 2
- Email 3: Day 4
- Email 4: Day 7
- Email 5: Day 10
- Email 6: Day 14
- Email 7: Day 21

---

## EMAIL 1: Welcome + Instant Value

**Subject:** Welcome to [BRAND] — here's where it starts

**Body:**

Hey [FIRST NAME],

Welcome. Seriously.

Most people sign up for things and never fully engage. You're different — you actually showed up.

Here's what you just got yourself into:

[BRAND] is where [CORE VALUE PROPOSITION]. We help people like you [MAIN OUTCOME].

Over the next few weeks, I'm going to show you exactly how we do it. No fluff. No generic advice. Just what works.

First question: what made you sign up?

Reply to this email. I read every one.

[YOUR NAME]
Founder, [BRAND]

---

## EMAIL 2: The Origin Story (Day 2)

**Subject:** Why I built this (and who it's for)

**Body:**

Hey,

I want to be straight with you.

I didn't build [BRAND] because I wanted to start a company. I built it because I had a problem and couldn't find a good enough solution.

[SHORT PERSONAL STORY — 2-3 sentences about WHY you started. Make it human. Make it real.]

So if you're the kind of person who:
- Is tired of half-baked solutions
- Wants something that actually works
- Values their time and wants results, not hype

Then you're in the right place.

If you're looking for magic pills and get-rich-quick schemes, this isn't for you. But if you want to do the work and get the results — stick around.

More tomorrow.

[YOUR NAME]

In my work with [AUDIENCE TYPE], I've found that almost everyone is missing one of these 3 things:

**1. [Framework Point 1]**
[1-2 sentence explanation of why it matters]

**2. [Framework Point 2]**
[1-2 sentence explanation of why it matters]

**3. [Framework Point 3]**
[1-2 sentence explanation of why it matters]

Most people have none of these. Some have one. The people who get results have all three.

If you want a deeper dive into any of these, reply and tell me which one resonates most. I read every email and I respond to the interesting ones.

[YOUR NAME]

## EMAIL 5: The Main Offer Introduction (Day 10)

**Subject:** What I offer (and whether it's right for you)

**Body:**

Hey,

I've been giving you value for 10 days. I want to make sure you know what I actually offer — in case you're ready to go deeper.

Here's what working with me looks like:

[PRODUCT/SERVICE NAME]
[Brief description — 2-3 sentences. What is it? Who is it for? What does it include?]

The reason I do this: [ONE SENTENCE ON YOUR WHY]

If it's a fit, I'd love to have you. If it's not, no hard feelings — there's still value in the emails you've already received.

Either way, I'm here.

[YOUR NAME]

---

## EMAIL 6: The Case Study (Day 14)

**Subject:** How one customer went from [PAIN POINT] to [RESULT]

**Body:**

Hey,

I want to tell you a story.

[NAME] was [BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF WHO THEY WERE — their situation, their pain point]. They'd tried [WHAT THEY TRIED BEFORE] and nothing worked.

Within [TIMEFRAME], they'd [WHAT THEY ACHIEVED]. Here's what they said:

*"[TESTIMONIAL QUOTE — specific and results-focused]"*

What changed? They stopped looking for shortcuts and committed to doing the work.

I can't do the work for you. But I can show you exactly what work to do.

If you're ready: [LINK TO OFFER]

If you're not there yet, keep reading. I'm still here.

[YOUR NAME]

---

## EMAIL 7: The Final Nudge (Day 21)

**Subject:** A question before I let you go

**Body:**

Hey,

I've enjoyed having you around.

I won't send you emails forever — I want to be respectful of your inbox. But before I do, I want to ask:

Is there something holding you back from [YOUR MAIN OUTCOME]?

Seriously. Reply and tell me.

It could be:
- Cost ("I can't afford it")
- Time ("I don't have time")
- Trust ("I don't know if this will work")
- Something else entirely

Whatever it is, I want to hear it. Because if it's something I can help with, I will. And if it's not a fit, I'd rather know now than keep sending you emails you don't need.

No pressure. Just honesty.

Reply and tell me what's stopping you.

[YOUR NAME]
Founder, [BRAND]

## CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

Replace all bracketed text:

**Core Info:**
- `[BRAND]` = Your business name
- `[YOUR NAME]` = Your name
- `[FIRST NAME]` = Use merge tag {customer_first_name} if available
- `[CORE VALUE PROPOSITION]` = What you do in one line
- `[MAIN OUTCOME]` = The result you deliver

**Audience:**
- `[AUDIENCE TYPE]` = Who you serve (e.g., "small business owners", "e-commerce sellers")

**Offer:**
- `[PRODUCT/SERVICE NAME]` = Name of your offer
- `[LINK TO OFFER]` = URL to your sales page/checkout

**Social Proof:**
- `[X]` = Number of customers (even if small, use it)
- `[TESTIMONIAL 1/2/3]` = Real testimonials with specific results
- `[NAME]` = Customer first name or initials

**Case Study:**
- `[PAIN POINT]` = What they struggled with before
- `[RESULT]` = What they achieved after

# Abandoned Cart Recovery — 5-Email E-Commerce Sequence

*The emails that bring customers back and close the sale.*

---

## Overview

This is a 5-email abandoned cart recovery sequence designed for e-commerce brands. These emails are triggered when a customer adds items to their cart but leaves without purchasing.

**Use case:** Any e-commerce store (fashion, beauty, home, tech, food, etc.)

**The math:** The average cart abandonment rate is 70-80%. Even recovering 10% of abandoned carts can add significant revenue.

**Timing:**
- Email 1: 30 minutes after abandonment
- Email 2: 4 hours later
- Email 3: 24 hours later
- Email 4: 48 hours later
- Email 5: 72 hours later

---

## EMAIL 1: The Gentle Reminder (30 Minutes)

**Subject:** You left something behind 🛒

**Body:**

Hey [FIRST NAME],

You added [PRODUCT NAME(S)] to your cart but didn't complete your purchase.

No pressure. Just a reminder — in case you got distracted.

Here's your cart link: [CART LINK]

If you have any questions while you're deciding, reply to this email. I (actually) read them.

[YOUR NAME]
[BRAND]

---

## EMAIL 2: The Urgency Email (4 Hours)

**Subject:** Heads up — [PRODUCT NAME] is running low

**Body:**

Hey,

I wanted to let you know: [PRODUCT NAME] is selling fast.

[IF APPLICABLE: We only have X left in stock.]

I don't believe in fake urgency. But I also don't want you to miss out if this is something you really want.

Your cart is still here: [CART LINK]

And if you have any questions — about sizing, shipping, returns, anything — I'm one email away.

[YOUR NAME]
[BRAND]

---

## EMAIL 3: The Value Reminder (24 Hours)

**Subject:** What you're missing (and why it matters)

**Body:**

Hey,

I know you looked at [PRODUCT NAME]. Here's why I think it's worth it:

[BENEFIT 1 — specific outcome]
[BENEFIT 2 — specific outcome]
[BENEFIT 3 — specific outcome]

You clearly saw the value or you wouldn't have added it to your cart in the first place.

Here's your link again: [CART LINK]

No rush. But don't wait too long — [ANY RELEVANT TIMELINE, e.g., "the next batch ships in X days" or "prices go up on X date"].

[YOUR NAME]
[BRAND]

---

## EMAIL 4: The Social Proof (48 Hours)

**Subject:** What [X] people say about [PRODUCT NAME]

**Body:**

Hey,

I wanted to share what some of our customers said about [PRODUCT NAME]:

*"[TESTIMONIAL 1 — specific and real]"*

*"[TESTIMONIAL 2 — specific and real]"*

*"[TESTIMONIAL 3 — specific and real]"*

These are real people who took a chance and were glad they did.

Your cart is still waiting: [CART LINK]

[YOUR NAME]
[BRAND]

---

## EMAIL 5: The Final Chance (72 Hours)

**Subject:** One last thing...

**Body:**

Hey,

I've sent you a few emails about [PRODUCT NAME]. I won't keep bothering you after this.

Before you go — I want to be honest about something.

[CHOOSE ONE:]
A) We're discontinuing this color/size soon and won't restock.
B) The price is going up [DATE].
C) This is the last one we have in your size.
D) This offer expires [DATE].

If it's not right for you, that's completely fine. But if you've been on the fence, now's the time.

[CART LINK]

Either way, thanks for considering us.

[YOUR NAME]
[BRAND]

P.S. If you need anything else, just reply. I read every email and I respond.

## CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

**Required replacements:**
- `[FIRST NAME]` = Use merge tag {customer_first_name}
- `[PRODUCT NAME(S)]` = Name of the product(s) in cart
- `[CART LINK]` = Direct link to customer's cart
- `[BRAND]` = Your brand/store name
- `[YOUR NAME]` = Your name or store name

**Testimonials:**
- Replace with real customer testimonials
- If you don't have testimonials yet, use these placeholders and add real ones as you collect them:
  - *"Fit perfectly. Wore it three times in the first week."*
  - *"Exactly what I was looking for. Should've bought sooner."*

**Urgency (Email 4):**
- Only use genuine urgency. If you have limited stock or a real deadline, use it.
- If there's no real urgency, skip the urgency angle and focus on value.

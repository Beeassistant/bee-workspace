# Post-Purchase Follow-Up — 5-Email E-Commerce Sequence

*Turn one-time buyers into repeat customers and brand advocates.*

---

## Overview

This is a 5-email post-purchase sequence designed for e-commerce brands. These emails are sent after a customer completes a purchase, focusing on enhancing their experience, encouraging reviews, and driving repeat business.

**Use case:** Any e-commerce store looking to build customer loyalty and increase Lifetime Customer Value (LTV).

**The goal:** Move beyond the transaction. Build a relationship. Turn buyers into fans.

**Timing:**
- Email 1: Immediately after purchase confirmation (can be combined with order confirmation)
- Email 2: Day 3 (product received / in-use prompt)
- Email 3: Day 7 (review request)
- Email 4: Day 14 (related product recommendation / upsell)
- Email 5: Day 30 (loyalty offer / re-purchase reminder)

---

## EMAIL 1: The Thank You + What's Next (Immediately)

**Subject:** Your [PRODUCT NAME] is on its way! 🎉

**Body:**

Hey [FIRST NAME],

Thank you for your recent purchase from [BRAND]! Your order #[ORDER NUMBER] is confirmed and your [PRODUCT NAME] is on its way.

You can track your order here: [TRACKING LINK]

In the meantime, we wanted to say a quick thank you. Every purchase helps us [MISSION/IMPACT, e.g., "continue crafting unique, sustainable pieces" or "support local artisans"].

We're so excited for you to experience [KEY BENEFIT OF PRODUCT].

If you have any questions, just reply to this email. We're here to help.

Warmly,
[YOUR NAME]
[BRAND]

---

## EMAIL 2: The Experience Enhancer (Day 3)

**Subject:** Get the most out of your [PRODUCT NAME]

**Body:**

Hey [FIRST NAME],

By now, your [PRODUCT NAME] should have arrived (or is very close!). We hope you're loving it.

To make sure you get the absolute best experience, here are a few quick tips:

*   **[Tip 1]:** [Brief explanation/instruction, e.g., "How to set it up," "Best practices for use." If it's a physical product, how to care for it.]
*   **[Tip 2]:** [Another tip to enhance their experience or reveal a hidden feature.]
*   **[Tip 3 (Optional)]:** [A final tip, or a link to a helpful resource like a video tutorial or FAQ.]

Got a question? Found a cool way to use it? Hit reply and let us know! We love hearing from you.

Happy [using your product],
[YOUR NAME]
[BRAND]

---

## EMAIL 3: The Review Request (Day 7)

**Subject:** We'd love your thoughts on [PRODUCT NAME] 🙏

**Body:**

Hey [FIRST NAME],

It's been about a week since your [PRODUCT NAME] arrived, and we're really eager to hear what you think!

Your honest feedback helps us improve and helps other customers make informed decisions.

Would you take just 60 seconds to leave a review?

[LINK TO LEAVE REVIEW]

It makes a huge difference to a small business like ours.

Thank you for your time!

Best,
[YOUR NAME]
[BRAND]

Based on your purchase, we thought you might also be interested in some complementary items that customers often buy together:

Explore them here: [LINK TO COLLECTION/NEW PRODUCTS]

Happy browsing!
[YOUR NAME]
[BRAND]

**Body:**

It's been about a month since you joined the [BRAND] family, and we just wanted to say thank you for being an amazing customer.

As a token of our appreciation, please enjoy **[DISCOUNT PERCENTAGE]% off your next order** with code **[DISCOUNT CODE]**.

This offer is valid for [X] days, so don't wait too long!

Shop now: [LINK TO STORE]

We're always striving to bring you [BRAND VALUE, e.g., "the best quality products," "innovative solutions"]. We hope to see you again soon!

Warmly,
[YOUR NAME]
[BRAND]

## CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

**Required replacements:**
- `[FIRST NAME]` = Use merge tag {customer_first_name}
- `[PRODUCT NAME]` = Name of the product purchased
- `[BRAND]` = Your brand/store name
- `[YOUR NAME]` = Your name or store name
- `[ORDER NUMBER]` = Customer's order number
- `[TRACKING LINK]` = Link to order tracking
- `[MISSION/IMPACT]` = Your brand's mission or positive impact
- `[KEY BENEFIT OF PRODUCT]` = Main benefit of the product they bought
- `[TIP 1/2/3]` = Specific tips for product use/care
- `[LINK TO LEAVE REVIEW]` = Direct link to your product review page
- `[RELATED PRODUCT NAMES]` = Names of complementary products
- `[LINK TO COLLECTION/NEW PRODUCTS]` = Link to product categories or new arrivals
- `[DISCOUNT PERCENTAGE]` = Your loyalty discount (e.g., 10, 15, 20)
- `[DISCOUNT CODE]` = The actual discount code (e.g., THANKYOU15)
- `[X] DAYS` = Expiry for the discount (e.g., 7 days)

---

**Tips for success:**
- **Personalize:** Use customer names and specific product details where possible.
- **Timing:** Adjust timings based on your typical shipping speed and product usage cycles.
- **Segment:** Consider different follow-up sequences for first-time vs. repeat buyers, or high-value vs. low-value purchases.

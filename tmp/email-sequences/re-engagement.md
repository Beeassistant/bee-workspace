# Re-Engagement Campaign — 4-Email Sequence for Inactive Subscribers

*Wake up your cold email list, prevent churn, and win back lost customers.*

---

## Overview

This is a 4-email re-engagement sequence designed to reconnect with inactive subscribers. These emails are triggered when a subscriber hasn't opened or clicked an email in a specific timeframe (e.g., 60-90 days).

**Use case:** Any business with an email list (e-commerce, SaaS, content creators, service providers).

**The goal:** Identify engaged subscribers, win back those who are still interested, and respectfully remove those who aren't, keeping your list healthy and maximizing deliverability.

**Timing:**
- Email 1: Day 0 (e.g., after 60 days of inactivity)
- Email 2: Day 3
- Email 3: Day 7
- Email 4: Day 10 (Final goodbye / last chance)

---

## EMAIL 1: The Check-In (Day 0)

**Subject:** Still with us? 👋

**Body:**

Hey [FIRST NAME],

It looks like it's been a while since we last heard from you. We've missed you!

We wanted to check in and make sure you're still interested in hearing from [BRAND].

We've been busy [mention 1-2 recent highlights, e.g., "launching new features," "sharing valuable tips," "expanding our product line"].

If you'd like to continue receiving updates from us, simply click here:

[LINK TO RE-ENGAGE / UPDATE PREFERENCES]

If not, no worries at all. We understand inboxes get crowded! Just ignore this email.

Thanks,
[YOUR NAME]
[BRAND]

---

## EMAIL 2: The Value Reminder (Day 3)

**Subject:** What you might have missed...

**Body:**

Hey [FIRST NAME],

Still thinking about you! We know life gets busy, and sometimes emails get lost in the shuffle.

Since you've been away, we've shared some pretty valuable [content type, e.g., "insights," "tips," "new products"] that we think you'd really appreciate, like:

*   **[Highlight 1]:** [Brief description of a valuable piece of content/feature. LINK]
*   **[Highlight 2]:** [Brief description of another valuable piece of content/feature. LINK]

Our goal is to help you [CORE BENEFIT/OUTCOME]. If you're still on that journey, we'd love for you to rejoin us.

Click here to stay subscribed: [LINK TO RE-ENGAGE]

Hope to see you back!
[YOUR NAME]
[BRAND]

---

## EMAIL 3: The Feedback Request / Final Offer (Day 7)

**Subject:** Quick question: how can we do better?

**Body:**

Hey [FIRST NAME],

One last time, we're reaching out because your feedback is important to us.

If you've been disengaged, we'd love to know why. What could we do differently to make our emails more valuable to you?

Reply to this email with your thoughts — seriously, we read every response.

Alternatively, if you're just looking for a reason to come back, how about this:

**Get [DISCOUNT PERCENTAGE]% off your next [PURCHASE TYPE] with code [DISCOUNT CODE]!**

This is a limited-time offer, just for you. Click here to claim it: [LINK TO OFFER]

We hope to win you back!

Best,
[YOUR NAME]
[BRAND]

---

## EMAIL 4: The Goodbye (Day 10)

**Subject:** It's time to say goodbye (for now)

**Body:**

Hey [FIRST NAME],

This is our final email to you in this series.

We haven't heard from you in a while, and we want to respect your inbox. So, if we don't hear back, we'll be removing you from our mailing list on [DATE, e.g., "in 48 hours" or "next week"].

If you'd like to stay subscribed and continue receiving [BRAND] updates, insights, and offers, simply click the link below:

[FINAL LINK TO RE-ENGAGE / UPDATE PREFERENCES]

Otherwise, we wish you all the best.

You can always re-subscribe on our website anytime you're ready.

Goodbye for now,
[YOUR NAME]
[BRAND]

---

## CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

**Required replacements:**
- `[FIRST NAME]` = Use merge tag {customer_first_name}
- `[BRAND]` = Your brand/store name
- `[YOUR NAME]` = Your name or store name
- `[MENTION 1-2 RECENT HIGHLIGHTS]` = Briefly describe recent updates/value
- `[LINK TO RE-ENGAGE / UPDATE PREFERENCES]` = A clear link for them to confirm continued subscription
- `[CONTENT TYPE]` = Type of content you share (e.g., "insights," "tips")
- `[HIGHLIGHT 1/2]` = Specific valuable content/features with links
- `[CORE BENEFIT/OUTCOME]` = Main value your brand provides
- `[DISCOUNT PERCENTAGE]` = Your re-engagement discount
- `[PURCHASE TYPE]` = What the discount applies to (e.g., "order," "subscription")
- `[DISCOUNT CODE]` = The actual discount code
- `[LINK TO OFFER]` = Direct link to the discount offer page
- `[DATE]` = Date of removal from list

---

**Tips for success:**
- **Segmentation:** Target subscribers based on their level of inactivity and engagement history.
- **Testing:** A/B test subject lines and email content to see what resonates most with your audience.
- **Value first:** Even in re-engagement, aim to provide value rather than just asking for something.
